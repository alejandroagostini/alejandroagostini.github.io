Software Architecture

 

The software architecture of the HRV2 system consists of two modules: the TREATMENT module that monitors the evolution of the treatment specified by the doctor and the EMERGENCY module that checks for critical unexpected event such as sudden drops or increments of the heart rate, risk of ventricular fibrillation, and risk of sudden cardiac arrest. To carry out these tasks using the HR signal and activity level as inputs, the system combines modern techniques of heart rate variability analysis (HRV) and artificial intelligence (AI).
