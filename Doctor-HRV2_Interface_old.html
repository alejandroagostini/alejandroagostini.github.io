The DOCTOR-HRV2 interface allows the doctor to easily interact with the HRV2 system and to monitor the patient parameters and the evolution of the treatment.

“Pause” button: pause the AI engine (and the patient simulation, in case the patient is simulated).


“Delete” button: delete patient.


“Edit” button: change patients basic health parameters (body mass index, age, etc.).


“Create new patient” button: create a new patient, see “create new patient” GUI below.

This panel shows the health state evolution of the patients as well as the history of alters sent to the patient, the family and the doctor. Click here for information about the health state and action definition.

Patient Management Panel

History of patient’s health state

DAY/HOUR: specification of the starting time (day and hour) for the action of the corresponding step of the treatment.

ACTION window: specification of the actions (medicine and activity) to be taken at each step of the treatments. Each value of the actions is specified using a color code:

STATE window: specification of the state of the patient at every time step. The state space consists of the number of arrhythmia events, the number of significant frequency events (bradycardia-tachycardia), the level of risk of sudden cardiac arrest, and the level of risk of ventricular fibrillation.

Monitor Window

The monitor window presents the evolution of the heart rate signal in a configurable time window.

The instruction window shows the instructions generated by the artificial intelligence kernel that infers the emergency situation and provides specific commands to the patient, family, hospital, and doctor according to the state of the patient.
