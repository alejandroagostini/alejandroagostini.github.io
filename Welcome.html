My current position is group leader in cognitive robotics and artificial intelligence at the department of Computational Neuroscience (Bernstein Center for Computational Neuroscience - University of Göttingen). My field of research covers robotic cognitive architectures, task planning, human-machine interaction, function approximation in reinforcement learning, dynamic programming, planning operator learning, and artificial intelligence for prediction and decision-making in life science. My current research is focused on probabilistic task planning and symbol grounding for robotic cognitive architectures as well as big-data analysis for prediction and decision-making in healthcare and precision agriculture.
