Edit/Create Patient

 

The interface for creating/editing a patient allows the doctor to create a new (simulated or real) patient or to change the basic health parameters of an existing one.

Basic health parameters of the patient: age, body mass index (BMI), no HDL cholesterol, fasting plasma glucose (FPG), and gender. The panel conditions permits specifying if the patient has hypertension (HTA), diabetes, a cardiovascular disease (e.g. coronary), and if the patients smokes. The possible values of these parameters are specified in the health state definition page.


These parameters are relevant to determine the chronic risk of the patient following the guidelines of the score risk charts of the European Society of Cardiology.

The activity and medicine panel allows configuring the activity to be performed and the medicine intake for a simulated patient. The charts are interactive. It is possible to configure the activity by clicking and dragging with the mouse, or by selecting specific time stamps for medicine intake.
