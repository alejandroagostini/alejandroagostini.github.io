SIRVENT (National project. Spanish research council. 2003-2006): Reconfigurable System for Vision Based Navigation of Legged and Wheeled Robots in Natural Environments. Development of  reinforcement learning algorithms for navigation tasks of hexapod robots in unstructured environments.

GARNICS (STREP EU Project. 2010-2013). GARdening with a Cognitive System (www.garnics.eu). Development of machine learning and planning approaches to control systems with a long and varying temporal delays between causes and effects. Implementation of a decision-making framework that integrates all the mechanisms for learning and planning in tasks related to plant caring.

INTELLACT (STREP EU Project. 2011-2014). INTELLigent  observation and execution of ACTions and manipulations (www.intellact.eu). Work Package leader. Development of machine learning methods to extract action rules from observations of action executions, coded as semantic event chains, and to implement a decision-making framework to close the planning-learning loop.

PACO-PLUS (IP EU Project. 2006-2010). Perception, Action, and Cognition through learning Object-Action Complexes (www.paco-plus.org). Development and integration of task planning and machine learning techniques for the rapid learning and use for decision-making of planning operators generated during the execution of tasks under the guidance of a human teacher.

XPERIENCE (IP EU Project. 2011-2016). Robots Bootstrapped through Learning from Experience (www.xperience.org). Integration of planning and learning techniques for the robotic executions of human-like tasks in a kitchen scenario. Development of a task planning method with missing objects substitution based on affordance evaluation.
