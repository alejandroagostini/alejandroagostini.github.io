The PATIENT-HRV2 interface informs the patient about the action to take in case of risk of cardiovascular event and the evolution of the current treatment.

Current action to be executed by the patient. After the action is executed the patient should press the button ‘CONFIRM’. In the window it is also specified the current time with respect to the initiation of the treatment.

Next action to be executed and the corresponding time.

Emergency buttons to call an ambulance, the doctor, or the relative selected to take care of emergencies. Note that the system will also produce automatic calls if it detects emergency events.

Interactive face that tells (audio) the patient which action to take and shows emotions depending on how fast the action is taken.

This window keeps track of the successful and failed execution of actions.

Sequence of actions to be taken during the treatment, specified using a color code.

Report of failed actions during the treatment.
