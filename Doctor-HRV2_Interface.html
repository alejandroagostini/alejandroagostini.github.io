Doctor-HRV2 Web Interface

The DOCTOR-HRV2 web interface allows the doctor to easily access to the health state evolution of the patients and to configure the HRV2 system.

This panel shows the history of health events of the patients as well as the history of alters sent to the patient, family and doctor. Click here for information about the health state and alert definition.

“Pause” button: pauses the AI engine (and the patient simulation, in case the patient is simulated).


“Delete” button: deletes patient recordings from the central database.


“Edit” button: changes patients basic health parameters (body mass index, age, etc.).


“Create new patient” button: creates a new patient, see “create new patient” GUI below. Click here to see how to create a new patient.

This panel shows the instructions generated by the artificial intelligence engine that sends specific alerts to the patient, family, and doctor according to the estimated acute risk.
